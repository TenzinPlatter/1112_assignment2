LOGIN    Login to your account. Only one person can be logged into an
         account at a time

REGISTER    Register an account for yourself. Cannot register an account with
            the same name as an already existing account. This will not log you
            into the account, that must be done seperately


!!!THE BELOW COMMANDS REQUIRE YOU TO BE LOGGED IN!!!

ROOMLIST    See a list of rooms available to join as either a player or viewer

CREATE    Create a room. This will not join the room, that must be done seperately

JOIN    Join a room as either a player or viewer
